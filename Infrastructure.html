AIMS AND OBJECTIVES
AKB Global School

The primary aim of St. Anthony's Sr. Sec. School is to realize the Education Vision of the Institute by imparting integral formation (religious, moral, social, cultural and physical) based on principles of Christian faith, to the members of the Christian Community.

It also imparts integral education (spiritual, mental, moral, cultural and physical) to all other girls admitted to this institution irrespective of caste or race.

INFRASTRUCTURE

AKB Global School is one of the leading schools in Delhi. The school provides state of the art infrastructure and amenities. The building accommodates spacious and well ventilated classrooms, seminar halls, activity halls, a huge library with collection of books, medical room, and state of the art fully equipped latest separate physics, chemistry, biology, computer, English rooms.

Class Rooms –

The classrooms are IT enabled and equipped with audio visual aids like smart boards, projectors etc that make learning interactive and enjoyable.

School Library –

The School Library has over 13,000 books and about 15-20 periodicals that come on a regular basis.The library remains open from 7:50 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. for students and staff use. A fully automated centre with a catalogue, wide range of resources, computers, CD Roms and cassettes along with a book covering a wide range of subjects.

School Labs-

We have state-of-art-of-education keeping pace with the global trends in the field of education with well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Computer labs to encourage learning by doing.

Sports-

The school has facilities for all games and extra-curricular activities with inter-class and inter-house contests to encourage healthy competition and to broaden horizons and build character. The school has excellent sports equipment and gymnasium facilities which includes a basketball court, badminton–court, play fields, table tennis room etc.
